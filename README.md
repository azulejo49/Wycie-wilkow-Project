## 🐺 The "Apex" Philosophy
In ecology, the howl of the wolf signifies the presence of an **"apex predator"**—a keystone species that regulates the entire food web, allowing forests to regenerate and rivers to flow naturally. 

**Project Wycie Wilków** deploys a Central AI as a "Digital Apex Predator." It does not conquer nature; it orchestrates and restores the natural balance across Poland's diverse biomes, meticulously merging ancient ecological wisdom (biomimicry, passive physics) with autonomous modern technology (Edge AI, ML, Hyperspectral Imaging).

---

## 🗺️ The Four Domains of Restoration (Biomes)

### 🌲 1. Sylva (The Primeval Forest)(WILK)
* **Location:** Białowieża National Park
* **The Crisis:** Spruce bark beetle (*Ips typographus*) infestations exacerbated by climate droughts, triggering highly destructive "salvage logging" policies.
* **The Ancient Method:** Dead-Wood Ecology and draft-horse extraction to prevent soil compaction.
* **The AI Integration:** The **"Silent Canopy" Protocol**. Fusing high-orbit multi-spectral satellite data (Sentinel-2) to detect early "Green Attacks" via water-stress signatures. At ground level, offline **AudioMoth Edge AI** sensors listen for the ultrasonic chewing of bark beetles without disturbing Natura 2000 protected birds. 

### 💧 2. Woda (Hydrological Lifelines)(ZUBR)
* **Location:** Masovian Plains & Central Poland
* **The Crisis:** Severe national freshwater scarcity, rapid snowmelt runoff, and devastating summer droughts due to historical river straightening.
* **The Ancient Method:** *Zastawki* (woven-wood weirs) and Beaver-mimicry to slow water flow and recharge aquifers.
* **The AI Integration:** Hydrological ML processing identifies optimal micro-choke points across thousands of small streams. Biodegradable, IoT-actuated *zastawki* are dynamically opened/closed by the Central AI based on IMGW rainfall forecasting, turning the country into a massive decentralized water sponge.

### ⛰️ 3. Ziemia (Industrial Detoxification)(dzięcioł)
* **Location:** Upper Silesia
* **The Crisis:** Toxic coal mining tailing heaps (*hałdy*) leaching heavy metals into the groundwater and releasing toxic coal dust.
* **The Ancient Method:** Phytoremediation and topographical terracing.
* **The AI Integration:** Chemical ML modeling calculates optimal geometric terracing for each specific heap. Autonomous **Drone Swarms** spray custom bio-mixtures of native hyper-accumulating plants (willows/mustards) and mycorrhizal fungi to biologically lock down the toxins.

### 🌊 4. Morze (The Baltic Defense)(łoś)
* **Location:** Pomeranian Coastline & Gdańsk Deep
* **The Crisis:** Coastal erosion, eutrophication (nitrogen-driven algal blooms), and rusting WWII chemical munitions.
* **The Ancient Method:** Permeable Baltic willow fascines, glacial boulder reefs, and biological filtration via native bivalves and shoreline reeds.
* **The AI Integration:** Hydrodynamic ML models simulate winter storm surges to 3D-print mathematically perfect fascine reefs. Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) seed these structures with **Baltic Blue Mussels (*Omułek jadalny*)** for bio-filtration, while AI-routed pontoons harvest shoreline reeds to extract excess nitrogen.
